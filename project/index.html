NMSRC

NMSRC is a research community of renowned scholars in the field of non-market strategy and related management fields.

Introduction

In 2018, we started as an informal group of scholars to share research ideas and organize scholarly activities at various academic conferences such as the Academy of Management (AOM) and the Strategic Management Society (SMS).

As we grew larger, we began to initiate various academic as well as social activities. We organized our very first brownbag seminar among our community members in 2019 and held two to three seminars every semester ever since, with expanded audiences. We also take efforts to support doctoral students engaged in non-market strategy research in various ways, and organized our first doctoral conference online in May 2021.

We look forward to continually engaging in various scholarly activities with academic and professional communities in the future.

02 Members

NMSRC Members

We are a growing community of scholars with diverse academic and professional backgrounds who are enthusiastic about the field of non-market strategy and who sincerely care about the next generation of academic scholars researching the topic. Our members engage in cutting-edge research at many different institutions around the globe and contribute to the field in various ways — as authors, as members of editorial review boards in top-tier academic journals, as editors, and as leaders in many academic organizations. 03 Academic Work

Academic Work

Our members engage in academic research centered on non-market strategy including, but not limited to, political strategy, corporate social responsibility, stakeholder strategy, business–government relations, private politics, social movements, non-profit organizations, regulation, innovation, institutions, and global strategy. Our work is published in top academic journals and leading practitioner journals and contributes to the field of non-market strategy. You can find our past and on-going works in this section.
