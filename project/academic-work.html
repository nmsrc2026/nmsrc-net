Academic
Work

Our members engage in academic research centered on non-market strategy — including, but not limited to, political strategy, corporate social responsibility, stakeholder strategy, business–government relations, private politics, social movements, non-profit organizations, regulation, innovation, institutions, and global strategy. Our work is published in top academic journals and leading practitioner journals and contributes to the field of non-market strategy.
